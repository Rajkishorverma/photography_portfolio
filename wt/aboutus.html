ABOUT US

Founded in 2022, Techphotos is a photography company located in Mumbai. It offers professional photography and videography services for weddings, events, products and e-commerce platforms. The idea of a pixel-based image and the stunning locations where we shoot photographs served as the inspiration for the creation of Techphotos.

Techphotos aims to build a photography agency that caters to all kinds of clients in the industry with a team of experienced and competent photographers and cinematographers capturing all the amazing and spellbinding moments of your ceremonies by combining them in a stunning album for you to cherish for a lifetime. To capture your events with the utmost perfection, we use cutting-edge equipment and high-quality lenses. The icing on the cake is the proficient shooting technique, which is innovative and artistic. pixQ is the best option for you if you’re searching for all-inclusive packages that may accommodate any necessity and budget size. We offer a wide range of photography and cinematography services, including the following,

  • Pre-wedding shoots
  • Wedding photography and videography
  • Candid Photography
  • Traditional photography and videography
  • Cinematography
  • Commercial photography (including product, e-commerce, fashion, corporate and interior shoot)

We promise expertise, imagination, and creative problem-solving to every project, assisting in the capturing of the photographs you require and those you simply cannot go without.
